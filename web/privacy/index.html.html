Privacy Policy – EscapadasGO API

Last updated: March 2025

1. Overview

This application (“EscapadasGO API”) is used exclusively by the owner for internal automation purposes, such as generating social media content and publishing travel information on the EscapadasGO brand channels.

2. Personal data collected

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from external users through this application. The app only interacts with Instagram and Facebook assets that are owned and managed by the business “Escapadas Mallorca” / “EscapadasGO”.

Any user data handled by Meta (Facebook/Instagram) is processed directly on Meta’s platforms in accordance with their own terms and policies.

3. How the API is used

The EscapadasGO API connects programmatically to Instagram and Facebook in order to:

  • Create and publish posts or Reels on business accounts owned by EscapadasGO.
  • Retrieve basic insights and statistics on the performance of those posts.

The app is not offered to third parties and is not used by external clients or users.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any information obtained through this application with third parties. Access to the app and its configuration is restricted to the internal team managing EscapadasGO channels.
